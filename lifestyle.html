Navigating college life at the University of Florida can be exciting but also overwhelming. Here are some key lifestyle tips to help you maintain a balanced, healthy, and fulfilling life both on and off campus.

Fitness & Wellness

If you're looking to stay active and healthy while at UF, check out these nearby gyms and outdoor activities!

Best Nearby Gyms

  • Southwest Recreation Center (SW Rec) – UF’s largest fitness facility with group fitness classes, weightlifting, and cardio equipment.
  • Student Recreation & Fitness Center – A convenient on-campus gym for quick workouts between classes.
  • Gainesville Health & Fitness – Off-campus option with a variety of amenities, including personal training.

Outdoor Activities

  • Lake Wauburg – Free for UF students; enjoy kayaking, paddleboarding, and hiking.
  • Depot Park – A scenic place for running, walking, or yoga.
  • Hawthorne Trail – Ideal for long bike rides and nature walks.

Nutrition: Meal Prepping on a Budget

Save money while eating healthy! Here are some grocery shopping tips and affordable meal prep ideas.

Grocery Shopping Tips

  • Shop at: Trader Joe’s, Aldi, Publix (BOGO deals!), Ward’s Supermarket (local produce).
  • Buy in Bulk: Rice, beans, oats, frozen veggies from Sam’s Club or Costco.
  • Student Discounts: Some grocery stores offer discounts with a student ID.

Easy & Affordable Meal Prep Ideas

  • Breakfast: Overnight oats, Greek yogurt with granola, protein smoothies.
  • Lunch: Chicken or tofu with brown rice and steamed veggies.
  • Dinner: Stir-fry with quinoa, beans, or lentils.
  • Snacks: Nuts, hummus & veggies, granola bars.

Maintaining a Routine

Stay organized and productive with these helpful tips!

Use a Planner

  • Apps like Notion, Google Calendar, or a physical planner can help organize tasks.

Set Priorities

  • Focus on 2-3 main tasks daily to avoid burnout.

Create a Morning & Night Routine

  • Helps regulate sleep and productivity.

Mental Health: Maintaining a Healthy Mindset

Take care of your mental health with these helpful resources and self-care tips.

UF Mental Health Resources

  • UF Counseling & Wellness Center (CWC): Offers free therapy and workshops.
  • GatorWell Health Promotion Services: Provides stress management resources and wellness coaching.
  • UF Mindfulness Program: Helps with meditation and self-care practices.

Self-Care Tips

  • Journaling: Helps process emotions and track mental health.
  • Meditation Apps: Try Calm or Headspace for daily mindfulness exercises.
  • Sleep Hygiene: Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep per night.

How to Destress Off-Campus & Maintain Work/Life Balance

Relax and unwind, while maintaining a healthy work/life balance with these tips.

Relaxing Locations & Cafés

  • Curia on the Drag: A cozy café with great coffee and ambiance.
  • Wyatt’s Coffee: A quiet spot for studying or unwinding.
  • Paynes Prairie Preserve: A peaceful place to enjoy nature and unwind.
  • Kanapaha Botanical Gardens: Perfect for an afternoon stroll and fresh air.
  • The Hippodrome Theatre: Great for catching a play or indie film.

Work/Life Balance Tips

  • Time Blocking: Dedicate specific hours to study, work, and relaxation.
  • Limit Overcommitment: Involvement in clubs is great, but don’t overextend yourself.
  • Make Time for Socializing: Balance is key—plan fun outings with friends.
  • Use Campus Resources: Career Connections Center for job guidance, Study Edge for academic support.
