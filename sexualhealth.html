What is consent?

Consent is about communication and respect. Consent is agreement or permission expressed through affirmative, voluntary words or actions that are mutually understandable to all parties involved, to engage in a specific sexual act at a specific time: Consent can be withdrawn at any time, as long as it is clearly communicated. Consent cannot be coerced or compelled by force, threat, deception or intimidation. Consent cannot be given by someone who is incapacitated, as defined below. Consent cannot be assumed based on silence, the absence of “no” or “stop,” the existence of a prior or current relationship, or prior sexual activity.

Understanding the Menstrual Cycle

From period symptoms to hormonal changes, get insights on how the menstrual cycle affects the body and mind.

Periods can cause discomfort, ranging from cramps to mood changes.

Hormonal Changes:

Hormones fluctuate throughout the cycle, impacting mood, energy, and other factors.

The Cycle:

Birth Control Options

Explore different birth control methods, their effectiveness, and potential side effects.

Methods:

There are various methods, including pills, IUDs, and condoms.

Side Effects:

Each method has potential side effects, from weight changes to mood swings.

Effectiveness:

Different methods have different effectiveness rates, with some being more reliable than others.

Hormonal Health

Understand how hormones impact mood, energy levels, and overall well-being.

Mood:

Hormonal fluctuations can cause changes in mood, including irritability and depression.

Energy:

Hormones can impact your energy levels, affecting both physical and mental performance.

Well-being:

A balanced hormonal system is key to maintaining overall health and well-being.
